Terms & Conditions and Academic Integrity

Key terms

  • All fees, timelines and scope of work are agreed in writing before support begins.
  • Payments are for time and expertise, not for grades or specific outcomes.
  • We reserve the right to decline work that conflicts with academic integrity or professional standards.

Academic integrity policy

Your university’s academic integrity rules always take priority. You remain responsible for your own submissions and for understanding how external support may be used.

  • You must not present any work created by another person as your own original submission.
  • Our comments, edits and examples are for learning and improvement; you decide what to adopt or change.
  • Similarity and AI checks are indicative only and cannot guarantee a particular Turnitin score or system response.
  • If your institution restricts the use of external support, you must follow those rules.

By using our services, you confirm that you will use all feedback and guidance in a way that is consistent with your institution’s regulations and that you remain fully responsible for how your work is submitted and assessed.
