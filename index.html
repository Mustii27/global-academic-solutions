How it works

A simple, transparent process designed around your deadlines. All support is provided online and scheduled around your availability.

Share your brief

Send the assignment or dissertation guidelines, marking rubric, word count and deadline. The more detail you provide, the more targeted our support can be.

Receive a plan & quote

We outline a clear plan: focus areas, expected time, and a fixed price or hourly estimate. You decide whether to proceed before any work starts.

Work together

We deliver tutoring, feedback and editing according to the plan, sharing comments on your drafts and arranging online sessions where needed.

Final checks

Before you submit, we can provide a final proofread and (on request) a Turnitin‑style similarity and AI‑pattern review for your reassurance.
